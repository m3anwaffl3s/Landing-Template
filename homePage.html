About Us

Serving the South Carolina community, Carolina Construction has had the pleasure of renovating residential homes for over 20 years. In the same vein as our previous clients, we take pride in your home and the work put into it. Founder, Henry Cook instilled respecting the integrity of one’s property, above all, the residing family. The ability to address variating conditions and demands is imperative in order to better work for more diverse markets. We work directly with clients to ensure satisfaction. Carolina Construction contractors are well versed in this industry, flexible, and possess a current South Carolina contractors license. Using the finest materials we modernize homes leaving a gratifying impression.
